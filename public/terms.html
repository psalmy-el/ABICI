1. Om ABICI

ABICI ("vi", "vårt" eller "oss") är ett professionellt managementkonsultföretag som tillhandahåller strategisk rådgivning, huvudrevision, processoptimering, riskhantering, förändringsledning och prestandaförbättringstjänster. Vårt registrerade kontor ligger på Ledungsgatan 2, 217 74 Malmö, Sverige.

2. Tjänsteavtal

2.1 Tjänsteomfattning

Våra konsulttjänster inkluderar men är inte begränsade till:

  • Strategisk planering och affärsutveckling
  • Huvudrevision och efterlevnadsbedömning
  • Processoptimering och operativ excellens
  • Förändringsledning och organisatorisk transformation
  • Riskhantering och riskreduceringsstrategier
  • Prestandaförbättring och KPI-optimering

2.2 Tjänsteleverans

Alla tjänster kommer att levereras enligt de specifika villkoren i individuella serviceavtal eller arbetsspecifikationer. Tidslinjer för tjänsteleverans, milstolpar och leveranser kommer att tydligt definieras i projektspecifik dokumentation.

3. Kundansvar

Kunder förbinder sig att:

  • Tillhandahålla korrekt, fullständig och aktuell information som krävs för tjänsteleverans
  • Ge rimlig tillgång till personal, lokaler och system enligt behov
  • Utse lämplig personal att arbeta med vårt konsultteam
  • Fatta snabba beslut om rekommendationer och föreslagna lösningar
  • Följa alla tillämpliga lagar och förordningar i sin jurisdiktion

4. Professional Standards and Confidentiality

4.1 Professional Conduct

ABICI maintains the highest standards of professional conduct and ethical behavior in accordance with international consulting and auditing standards. Our consultants are bound by professional codes of conduct and continue professional development requirements.

4.2 Confidentiality

We maintain strict confidentiality regarding all client information, business processes, strategies, and data. Non-disclosure agreements may be executed for additional protection of sensitive information. This confidentiality obligation survives the termination of our service relationship.

5. Intellectual Property

5.1 Client IP

All pre-existing intellectual property rights of the client remain with the client. We respect and protect client proprietary information and trade secrets.

5.2 ABICI Methodologies

Our proprietary consulting methodologies, frameworks, tools, and templates remain our intellectual property. Clients receive a limited license to use deliverables created specifically for their engagement.

5.3 Work Product

Reports, analyses, recommendations, and other deliverables created specifically for a client engagement become the property of the client upon full payment of fees.

6. Fees and Payment Terms

6.1 Fee Structure

Fees are based on the complexity of the engagement, time required, and value delivered. Fee structures may include:

  • Fixed project fees
  • Time and materials basis
  • Retainer arrangements
  • Value-based pricing for specific outcomes

6.2 Payment Terms

Unless otherwise specified, payment terms are net 30 days from invoice date. Late payments may incur interest charges. We reserve the right to suspend services for overdue accounts.

7. Limitation of Liability

Our liability for any claims arising from our services is limited to the fees paid for the specific engagement giving rise to the claim. We are not liable for indirect, consequential, or punitive damages. This limitation applies to the fullest extent permitted by applicable law.

8. Termination

Either party may terminate an engagement with written notice. Upon termination, clients are responsible for payment of all services rendered through the termination date. Confidentiality obligations survive termination.

9. Dispute Resolution

Any disputes arising from our services will first be addressed through good faith negotiations. If unresolved, disputes will be subject to binding arbitration under Swedish arbitration rules, with proceedings conducted in English in Stockholm, Sweden.

10. Governing Law

These terms are governed by Swedish law. Any legal proceedings will be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of Swedish courts.
